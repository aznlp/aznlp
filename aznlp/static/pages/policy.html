Privacy Policy

Welcome to AZNLP (“AZNLP", "we", "us", and "our"). AZNLP champions AI technology to empower businesses globally, specializing in advanced language processing solutions. Our platform integrates AI and language technologies to offer innovative products and services that enhance business operations and communication. The “Website” refers to AZNLP’s website (including, but not limited to, www.aznlp.com and any successor URLs, mobile or localized versions, and related domains and subdomains), and the “Services” encompasses AZNLP’s products, applications, and services offered now or in the future.

Purpose of This Privacy Policy

This “Privacy Policy” aims to inform you about how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you interact with our Services. It outlines the types of data we collect, our purposes for processing your data, and your rights concerning your personal data. If you disagree with any part of this Privacy Policy, please refrain from using our Services.

Personal Data We Collect

Personal Data you provide to us:

To use AZNLP’s Services, you may need to provide personal information such as your name, email address, and telephone number. For premium services, payment information will be required, handled securely by our payment service providers. You may also choose to provide additional profile information voluntarily, like a profile photo or timezone. We will clearly communicate when certain information is mandatory and the implications of not providing it. Customer support interactions may also be stored, including contact details and issue descriptions, to help resolve any problems effectively.

Automatically collected data:

We collect information automatically via cookies and other tracking technologies to enhance user experience and improve our Services. This includes data such as IP addresses, browser types, and visit timestamps. Our “Cookies” section below further details our use of these technologies.

Data from integrated services:

When you link third-party services to your AZNLP account, we access data from those services to provide our integrated language processing solutions. This could include accessing emails, messages from instant messaging applications, or documents from third-party services, depending on the permissions and privacy settings of those services. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you integrate with our Services.

Data Security

AZNLP takes robust precautions and employs both organizational and technical measures to safeguard your Personal Data. Our aim is to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. This commitment extends to our use of secure payment systems and adherence to best practices and legal standards for data protection.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our Service and hold certain information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier. Cookies are sent to your browser from a website and stored on your device. Other tracking technologies also used are beacons, tags, and scripts to collect and track information and to improve and analyze our Service.

Your Data Protection Rights

AZNLP respects your privacy rights and provides you with reasonable access to the Personal Data that you may have provided through your use of the Services. Your principal rights under data protection law are: the right to access, the right to rectification, the right to erasure, the right to restrict processing, the right to object to processing, the right to data portability, the right to complain to a supervisory authority, and the right to withdraw consent. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us. We will promptly review and address your request in accordance with applicable laws.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
